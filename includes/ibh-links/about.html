Aims and Objectives



The goals of Odeyemmy OS na it dey listed below:

To improve the user-experience of all the Nigerian citizen and Engineering students. Na wetin make we provide support for vernacular.

To provide the in-depth knowledge wey dey needed on CISCO Networking concepts.

To enable network administrators dey watch over their network and prevent attacks from hackers.

To dey help in packet-analysis and to dey reveal the event(s) in real time wey dey over a network environment connected to the Internet.
